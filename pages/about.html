About Atom Technologies

I've always had a passion for coding and technology, which naturally led me to explore game development.
My journey into the world of gaming began with a desire to learn the Unity game engine.
As a self-taught coder, I was eager to dive into game design and see what I could
create using this powerful platform.

In late 2022 I created Atom Technologies and registered the name to my Microsoft Developer account.
I then released my first game, Reverse Snake, in mid-October of the same year.
What started as a small curiosity project quickly grew as I began receiving more downloads,
which inspired me to expand the business.
Over time, Atom Technologies evolved into an exciting hobby that I'm passionate about.

As the business grew, I also began to leverage social media platforms like Instagram and YouTube
to engage with my audience. These platforms have been invaluable in connecting
with players, sharing updates, and showcasing new projects, helping Atom Technologies reach a wider community.

Since the release of Reverse Snake, I have expanded my game library. One of the early
follow-ups, In the Abyss, immersed players in a deep, atmospheric experience, emphasizing exploration and risk taking.
Next came Wild Eclipse, an adventure game that combined action with a rich storyline and stylized visuals.Following that,
Project Walker took on a more ambitious tone, blending survival elements with maze generation.
This game challenged players with more complex systems and even implemented pathfinding algorithms.
Finally, my favorite game of all,10 Second Escape presented a fast-paced experience.
In just ten seconds, players were tasked with parkouring there way to each checkpoint
Each of these titles has contributed to the evolution of Atom Technologies, refining my
skills as a game developer. I'm super exited to see where Atom technologies will end up!
